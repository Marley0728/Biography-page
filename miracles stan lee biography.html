The life and Legacy of Stanlee Martin Lieber

Stan Lee (1922-2018)

Who is Stan Lee

Stan Lee who's real name was Stanley Martin Lieber was an american comic book writer, editor, publisher and producer. He was Marvel's primary creative leaders for two decades, expanding it from a small publishing house to a multimedia corporation that dominated the comics and film industries.

Early life

He was born on December 28th, 1922, in Manhattan, Ney York City, in the appartment of his Romanian-born jewish immigrant parents, Celia and Jack Lieber at the corner of west 98th Street and West Avenue. Lee was raised in a jewish household.

Philanthropy

The Stan Lee Foundation was founded in 2010 to focus on literacy, education, and the arts. Its stated goals include supporting programs and ideas that improve access to literacy resources, as well as promoting diversity, national literacy, culture and the arts.

Death

Lee underwent an operation in September 2012 to insert a pacemaker, which required cancelling planned appearances at conventions. He died on November 12,2018, at Cedars-Sinai Medical Center in Los Angeles, after being rushed for a medical emergency earlier in the day. The immediate cause of feath listed on his death certificate was cardiac arrest with respiratory failure and congestive heart failure.
